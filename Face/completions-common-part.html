Face: completions-common-part (sample)

Documentation:
Face for the common prefix substring in completions. The idea of this face is that you can use it to make the common parts less visible than normal, so that the differing parts are emphasized by contrast. See also the face `completions-first-difference'.

Defined in `minibuffer.el'.
