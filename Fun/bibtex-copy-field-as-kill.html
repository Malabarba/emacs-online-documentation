bibtex-copy-field-as-kill is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `bibtex.el'.


(bibtex-copy-field-as-kill &optional COMMA)

Copy the BibTeX field at point to `bibtex-field-kill-ring'.
Optional arg COMMA is as in `bibtex-enclosing-field'. It is t for
interactive calls.
