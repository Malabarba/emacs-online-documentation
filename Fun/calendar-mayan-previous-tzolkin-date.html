--- title: "calendar-mayan-previous-tzolkin-date" layout: doc --- calendar-mayan-previous-tzolkin-date is an interactive autoloaded Lisp function in `cal-mayan.el'.


(calendar-mayan-previous-tzolkin-date TZOLKIN-DATE &optional NOECHO)

Move cursor to previous instance of Mayan TZOLKIN-DATE.
Echo Mayan date unless NOECHO is non-nil.
