--- title: "compose-gstring-for-variation-glyph" layout: doc --- compose-gstring-for-variation-glyph is a compiled Lisp function in `japanese.el'.

(compose-gstring-for-variation-glyph GSTRING)

Compose glyph-string GSTRING for graphic display.
GSTRING must have two glyphs; the first is a glyph for a han character,
and the second is a glyph for a variation selector.
