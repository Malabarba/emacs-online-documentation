--- title: "compose-mail-other-window" layout: doc --- compose-mail-other-window is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `simple.el'.

It is bound to C-x 4 m.

(compose-mail-other-window &optional TO SUBJECT OTHER-HEADERS CONTINUE YANK-ACTION SEND-ACTIONS RETURN-ACTION)

Like C-x m, but edit the outgoing message in another window.
