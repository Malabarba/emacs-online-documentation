describe-copying is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `help.el'.

It is bound to C-h C-c, <f1> C-c, <help> C-c, <menu-bar> <help-menu> <describe-copying>.

(describe-copying)

Display info on how you may redistribute copies of GNU Emacs.
