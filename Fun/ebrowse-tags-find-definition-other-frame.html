ebrowse-tags-find-definition-other-frame is an interactive autoloaded compiled Lisp function in `ebrowse.el'.

It is bound to C-c RET 5 f, C-c RET 5 ..

(ebrowse-tags-find-definition-other-frame)

Find definition of member at point in other frame.
