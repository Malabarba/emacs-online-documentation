--- title: "ediff-merge-buffers" layout: doc --- ediff-merge-buffers is an interactive autoloaded compiled Lisp function in `ediff.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <tools> <ediff-merge> <ediff-merge-buffers>.

(ediff-merge-buffers BUFFER-A BUFFER-B &optional STARTUP-HOOKS JOB-NAME MERGE-BUFFER-FILE)

Merge buffers without ancestor.
