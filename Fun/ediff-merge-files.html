ediff-merge-files is an interactive autoloaded compiled Lisp function in `ediff.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <tools> <ediff-merge> <ediff-merge-files>.

(ediff-merge-files FILE-A FILE-B &optional STARTUP-HOOKS MERGE-BUFFER-FILE)

Merge two files without ancestor.
