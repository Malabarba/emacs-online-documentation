--- title: "epa-info-mode" layout: doc --- epa-info-mode is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `epa.el'.


(epa-info-mode)

Parent mode: `special-mode'.

Major mode for `epa-info-buffer'.

In addition to any hooks its parent mode `special-mode' might have run,
this mode runs the hook `epa-info-mode-hook', as the final step
during initialization.
