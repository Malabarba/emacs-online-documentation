--- title: "gdb-inferior-io-mode" layout: doc --- gdb-inferior-io-mode is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `gdb-mi.el'.


(gdb-inferior-io-mode)

Parent mode: `comint-mode'.

Major mode for gdb inferior-io.

In addition to any hooks its parent mode `comint-mode' might have run,
this mode runs the hook `gdb-inferior-io-mode-hook', as the final step
during initialization.
