--- title: "gdb-toggle-switch-when-another-stopped" layout: doc --- gdb-toggle-switch-when-another-stopped is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `gdb-mi.el'.


(gdb-toggle-switch-when-another-stopped &optional INTERACTIVELY)

Toggle whether to switch to stopped thread.
In an interactive call, record this option as a candidate for saving
by "Save Options" in Custom buffers.
