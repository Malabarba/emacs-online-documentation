gnus-set-sorted-intersection is an alias for `gnus-sorted-nintersection' in `gnus-range.el'.

(gnus-set-sorted-intersection LIST1 LIST2)

Return intersection of LIST1 and LIST2 by modifying cdr pointers of LIST1.
LIST1 and LIST2 have to be sorted over <.
