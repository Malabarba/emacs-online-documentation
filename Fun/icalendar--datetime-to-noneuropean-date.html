icalendar--datetime-to-noneuropean-date is an alias for `icalendar--datetime-to-american-date' in `icalendar.el'.

(icalendar--datetime-to-noneuropean-date DATETIME &optional SEPARATOR)

This function is obsolete since icalendar 0.19;
use `icalendar--datetime-to-american-date' instead.

Convert the decoded DATETIME to American-style format.
Optional argument SEPARATOR gives the separator between month,
day, and year. If nil a blank character is used as separator.
American format: "month day year".
