insert-monthly-hebrew-diary-entry is an alias for `diary-hebrew-insert-monthly-entry' in `cal-hebrew.el'.


(insert-monthly-hebrew-diary-entry ARG)

This function is obsolete since 23.1;
use `diary-hebrew-insert-monthly-entry' instead.

Insert a monthly diary entry.
For the day of the Hebrew month corresponding to the date indicated by point.
Prefix argument ARG makes the entry nonmarking.
