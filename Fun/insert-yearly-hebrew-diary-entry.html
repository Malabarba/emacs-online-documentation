insert-yearly-hebrew-diary-entry is an alias for `diary-hebrew-insert-yearly-entry' in `cal-hebrew.el'.


(insert-yearly-hebrew-diary-entry ARG)

This function is obsolete since 23.1;
use `diary-hebrew-insert-yearly-entry' instead.

Insert an annual diary entry.
For the day of the Hebrew year corresponding to the date indicated by point.
Prefix argument ARG makes the entry nonmarking.
