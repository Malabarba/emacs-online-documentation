kmacro-insert-counter is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `kmacro.el'.

It is bound to C-x C-k TAB.

(kmacro-insert-counter ARG)

Insert macro counter, then increment it by ARG.
Interactively, ARG defaults to 1. With C-u, insert
previous `kmacro-counter', and do not modify counter.
