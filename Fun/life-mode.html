--- title: "life-mode" layout: doc --- life-mode is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `life.el'.


(life-mode)

Parent mode: `special-mode'.

Major mode for the buffer of `life'.

In addition to any hooks its parent mode `special-mode' might have run,
this mode runs the hook `life-mode-hook', as the final step
during initialization.
