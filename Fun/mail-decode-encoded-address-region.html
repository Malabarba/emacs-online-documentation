mail-decode-encoded-address-region is an alias for `rfc2047-decode-address-region' in `mail-parse.el'.

(mail-decode-encoded-address-region START END)

Decode MIME-encoded words in region between START and END.
Backslashes which precede characters other than `"' and `\' in quoted
strings are stripped.
