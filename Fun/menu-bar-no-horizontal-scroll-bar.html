--- title: "menu-bar-no-horizontal-scroll-bar" layout: doc --- menu-bar-no-horizontal-scroll-bar is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `menu-bar.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <options> <showhide> <showhide-scroll-bar> <none-horizontal>.

(menu-bar-no-horizontal-scroll-bar)

Turn off horizontal scroll bars.
