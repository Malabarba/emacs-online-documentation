--- title: "menu-bar-showhide-fringe-ind-box" layout: doc --- menu-bar-showhide-fringe-ind-box is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `menu-bar.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <options> <showhide> <showhide-fringe> <showhide-fringe-ind> <box>.

(menu-bar-showhide-fringe-ind-box)

Display top and bottom indicators in opposite fringes.
