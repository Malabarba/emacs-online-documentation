menu-bar-showhide-fringe-ind-customize is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `menu-bar.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <options> <showhide> <showhide-fringe> <showhide-fringe-ind> <customize>.

(menu-bar-showhide-fringe-ind-customize)

Show customization buffer for `indicate-buffer-boundaries'.
