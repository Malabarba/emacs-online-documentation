menu-bar-showhide-fringe-ind-right is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `menu-bar.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <options> <showhide> <showhide-fringe> <showhide-fringe-ind> <right>.

(menu-bar-showhide-fringe-ind-right)

Display buffer boundaries and arrows in the right fringe.
