menu-bar-showhide-fringe-menu-customize-reset is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `menu-bar.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <options> <showhide> <showhide-fringe> <default>.

(menu-bar-showhide-fringe-menu-customize-reset)

Reset the fringe mode: display fringes on both sides of a window.
