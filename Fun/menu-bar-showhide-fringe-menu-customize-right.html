--- title: "menu-bar-showhide-fringe-menu-customize-right" layout: doc --- menu-bar-showhide-fringe-menu-customize-right is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `menu-bar.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <options> <showhide> <showhide-fringe> <right>.

(menu-bar-showhide-fringe-menu-customize-right)

Display fringes only on the right of each window.
