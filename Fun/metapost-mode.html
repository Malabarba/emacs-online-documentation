--- title: "metapost-mode" layout: doc --- metapost-mode is an interactive autoloaded compiled Lisp function in `meta-mode.el'.


(metapost-mode)

Parent mode: `meta-common-mode'.

Major mode for editing MetaPost sources.

In addition to any hooks its parent mode `meta-common-mode' might have run,
this mode runs the hook `metapost-mode-hook', as the final step
during initialization.
