mm-multibyte-char-to-unibyte is an alias for `multibyte-char-to-unibyte' in `mm-util.el'.

(mm-multibyte-char-to-unibyte CH)

Convert the multibyte character CH to a byte.
If the multibyte character does not represent a byte, return -1.
