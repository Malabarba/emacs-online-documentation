mm-setup-codepage-iso-8859 is a compiled Lisp function in `mm-util.el'.

(mm-setup-codepage-iso-8859 &optional LIST)

Add appropriate entries to `mm-charset-synonym-alist'.
Unless LIST is given, `mm-codepage-iso-8859-list' is used.
