--- title: "mpc-status-mode" layout: doc --- mpc-status-mode is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `mpc.el'.


(mpc-status-mode)

Parent mode: `mpc-mode'.

Major mode to display MPC status info.

In addition to any hooks its parent mode `mpc-mode' might have run,
this mode runs the hook `mpc-status-mode-hook', as the final step
during initialization.
