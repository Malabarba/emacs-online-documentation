--- title: "org-list-get-first-item" layout: doc --- org-list-get-first-item is an alias for `org-list-get-list-begin' in `org-list.el'.

(org-list-get-first-item ITEM STRUCT PREVS)

Return point at beginning of sub-list ITEM belongs.
STRUCT is the list structure. PREVS is the alist of previous
items, as returned by `org-list-prevs-alist'.
