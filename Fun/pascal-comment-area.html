pascal-comment-area is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `pascal.el'.

(pascal-comment-area START END)

Put the region into a Pascal comment.
The comments that are in this area are "deformed":
`*)' becomes `!(*' and `}' becomes `!{'.
These deformed comments are returned to normal if you use
M-x pascal-uncomment-area to undo the commenting.

The commented area starts with `pascal-exclude-str-start', and ends with
`pascal-include-str-end'. But if you change these variables,
M-x pascal-uncomment-area won't recognize the comments.
