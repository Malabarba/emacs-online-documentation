--- title: "rfc2047-decode-address-region" layout: doc --- rfc2047-decode-address-region is a compiled Lisp function in `rfc2047.el'.

(rfc2047-decode-address-region START END)

Decode MIME-encoded words in region between START and END.
Backslashes which precede characters other than `"' and `\' in quoted
strings are stripped.
