rng-transform-group-nullable is a compiled Lisp function in `rng-match.el'.

(rng-transform-group-nullable F CONSER IPATTERN)

Given a group x1,...,xn,y1,...,yn where the xs are all
nullable and y1 isn't, return a choice
(conser f(x1) x2,...,xm,y1,...,yn)
|(conser f(x2) x3,...,xm,y1,...,yn)
|...
|(conser f(xm) y1,...,yn)
|(conser f(y1) y2,...,yn)
