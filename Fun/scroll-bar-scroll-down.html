scroll-bar-scroll-down is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `scroll-bar.el'.

It is bound to <vertical-scroll-bar> <drag-mouse-3>, <vertical-scroll-bar> <mouse-3>.

(scroll-bar-scroll-down EVENT)

Scroll the window's top line down to the location of the scroll bar click.
EVENT should be a scroll bar click.
