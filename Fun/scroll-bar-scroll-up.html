scroll-bar-scroll-up is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `scroll-bar.el'.

It is bound to <vertical-scroll-bar> <drag-mouse-1>, <vertical-scroll-bar> <mouse-1>.

(scroll-bar-scroll-up EVENT)

Scroll the line next to the scroll bar click to the top of the window.
EVENT should be a scroll bar click.
