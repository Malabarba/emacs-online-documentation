--- title: "shr-color-relative-to-absolute" layout: doc --- shr-color-relative-to-absolute is a compiled Lisp function in `shr-color.el'.

(shr-color-relative-to-absolute NUMBER)

Convert a relative NUMBER to absolute.
If NUMBER is absolute, return NUMBER.
This will convert "80 %" to 204, "100 %" to 255 but "123" to "123".
