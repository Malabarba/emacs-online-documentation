toggle-indicate-empty-lines is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `menu-bar.el'.

It is bound to <menu-bar> <options> <showhide> <showhide-fringe> <indicate-empty-lines>.

(toggle-indicate-empty-lines &optional INTERACTIVELY)

Toggle whether to indicate trailing empty lines in fringe, globally.
In an interactive call, record this option as a candidate for saving
by "Save Options" in Custom buffers.
