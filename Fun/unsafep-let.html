unsafep-let is a compiled Lisp function in `unsafep.el'.

(unsafep-let CLAUSE)

Check the safety of a let binding.
CLAUSE is a let-binding, either SYM or (SYM) or (SYM VAL).
Check VAL and throw a reason to `unsafep' if unsafe.
Return SYM.
