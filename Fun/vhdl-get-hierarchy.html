vhdl-get-hierarchy is a compiled Lisp function in `vhdl-mode.el'.

(vhdl-get-hierarchy ENT-ALIST CONF-ALIST ENT-KEY ARCH-KEY CONF-KEY CONF-INST-ALIST LEVEL INDENT &optional INCLUDE-TOP ENT-HIER)

Get instantiation hierarchy beginning in architecture ARCH-KEY of
entity ENT-KEY.
