--- title: "view-order-manuals" layout: doc --- view-order-manuals is an interactive compiled Lisp function in `help.el'.

It is bound to C-h RET, <f1> RET, <help> RET, <menu-bar> <help-menu> <more-manuals> <order-emacs-manuals>.

(view-order-manuals)

Display information on how to buy printed copies of Emacs manuals.
