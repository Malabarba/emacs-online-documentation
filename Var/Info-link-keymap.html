Info-link-keymap is a variable defined in `info.el'.
Its value is (keymap
(follow-link . mouse-face)
(mouse-2 . Info-mouse-follow-link)
(header-line keymap
(mouse-2 . Info-mouse-follow-link)
(mouse-1 . mouse-select-window)
(down-mouse-1 . mouse-drag-header-line)))


Documentation:
Keymap to put on Info links.
This is used for the "Next", "Prev", and "Up" links in the
first line or header line, and for breadcrumb links.
