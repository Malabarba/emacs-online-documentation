--- title: "ada-case-identifier" layout: doc --- ada-case-identifier is a variable defined in `ada-mode.el'.
Its value is 
ada-loose-case-word


Documentation:
Function to call to adjust the case of an Ada identifier.
It may be `downcase-word', `upcase-word', `ada-loose-case-word' or
`ada-capitalize-word'.

You can customize this variable.
