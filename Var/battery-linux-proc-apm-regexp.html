battery-linux-proc-apm-regexp is a variable defined in `battery.el'.
Its value is
"^\\([^ ]+\\) \\([^ ]+\\) 0x\\([0-9a-f]+\\) 0x\\([0-9a-f]+\\) 0x\\([0-9a-f]+\\) 0x\\([0-9a-f]+\\) \\(-?[0-9]+\\)% \\(-?[0-9]+\\) \\(.*\\)$"

This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Regular expression matching contents of `/proc/apm'.
