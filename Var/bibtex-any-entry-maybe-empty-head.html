bibtex-any-entry-maybe-empty-head is a variable defined in `bibtex.el'.
Its value is
"^[ ]*\\(@[ ]*[^\"#%'(),={} \n0-9][^\"#%'(),={} \n]*\\)[ ]*[({][ \n]*\\([][[:alnum:].:;?!`'/*@+|()<>&_^$-]+\\)?"

This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Regexp matching the header line of any BibTeX entry (possibly without key).
