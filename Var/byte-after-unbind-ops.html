byte-after-unbind-ops is a variable defined in `byte-opt.el'.
Its value is 


(byte-constant byte-dup byte-symbolp byte-consp byte-stringp byte-listp byte-numberp byte-integerp byte-eq byte-not byte-cons byte-list1 byte-list2 byte-interactive-p)

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.
