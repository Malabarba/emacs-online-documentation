c-recognize-paren-inits is a variable defined in `cc-engine.el'.
Its value is 
nil

  • Automatically becomes buffer-local when set.

Documentation:
Non-nil means that parenthesis style initializers exist,
i.e. constructs like

Foo bar (gnu);

in addition to the more classic

Foo bar = gnu;
