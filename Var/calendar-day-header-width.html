--- title: "calendar-day-header-width" layout: doc --- calendar-day-header-width is a variable defined in `calendar.el'.
Its value is 
2


Documentation:
Width of the day column headers in the calendar.
Must be at least one less than `calendar-column-width'.

You can customize this variable.

This variable was introduced, or its default value was changed, in version 23.1 of Emacs.
