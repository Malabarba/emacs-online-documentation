--- title: "copyright-years-regexp" layout: doc --- copyright-years-regexp is a variable defined in `copyright.el'.
Its value is 


"\\(\\s *\\)\\([1-9]\\([-0-9, ';/*%#\n	]\\|\\s<\\|\\s>\\)*[0-9]+\\)"


Documentation:
Match additional copyright notice years.
The second \( \) construct must match the years.

You can customize this variable.
