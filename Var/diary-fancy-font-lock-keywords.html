diary-fancy-font-lock-keywords is a variable defined in `diary-lib.el'.
Its value is 


((diary-fancy-date-matcher quote diary)
 ("^.*\\([aA]nniversary\\|[bB]irthday\\).*$" quote diary-anniversary)
 ("^.*Yahrzeit.*$" . font-lock-constant-face)
 ("^\\(Erev \\)?Rosh Hodesh.*" . font-lock-function-name-face)
 ("^Day.*omer.*$" . font-lock-builtin-face)
 ("^Parashat.*$" . font-lock-comment-face)
 ("\\(^\\|\\s-\\)[0-9]?[0-9]\\([AaPp][mM]\\|\\([Hh]\\([0-9][0-9]\\)?\\|[:.][0-9][0-9]\\)\\([AaPp][Mm]\\)?\\)\\(-[0-9]?[0-9]\\([AaPp][mM]\\|\\([Hh]\\([0-9][0-9]\\)?\\|[:.][0-9][0-9]\\)\\([AaPp][Mm]\\)?\\)\\)?" quote diary-time))

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.
