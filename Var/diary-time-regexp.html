diary-time-regexp is a variable defined in `diary-lib.el'.
Its value is 


"[0-9]?[0-9]\\([AaPp][mM]\\|\\([Hh]\\([0-9][0-9]\\)?\\|[:.][0-9][0-9]\\)\\([AaPp][Mm]\\)?\\)"

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.
