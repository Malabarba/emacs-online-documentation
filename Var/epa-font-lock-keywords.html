--- title: "epa-font-lock-keywords" layout: doc --- epa-font-lock-keywords is a variable defined in `epa.el'.
Its value is 


(("^\\*"
  (0 'epa-mark))
 ("^	\\([^	:]+:\\)[ 	]*\\(.*\\)$"
  (1 'epa-field-name)
  (2 'epa-field-body)))

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.
