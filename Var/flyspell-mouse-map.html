--- title: "flyspell-mouse-map" layout: doc --- flyspell-mouse-map is a variable defined in `flyspell.el'.
Its value is 


(keymap
 (mouse-2 . undefined)
 (down-mouse-2 . flyspell-correct-word))

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Keymap for Flyspell to put on erroneous words.
