--- title: "gnus-group-name-charset-group-alist" layout: doc --- gnus-group-name-charset-group-alist is a variable defined in `gnus-group.el'.
Its value is 
((".*" . utf-8))


Documentation:
Alist of group regexp and the charset for group names.

For example:
(("\.com\.cn:" . cn-gb-2312))

You can customize this variable.
