hfy-etag-regex is a variable defined in `htmlfontify.el'.
Its value is
".*\\([^\n]+\\)\\([0-9]+\\),\\([0-9]+\\)$\\|.*[0-9]+,[0-9]+$"

Documentation:
Regex used to parse an etags entry: must have 3 subexps, corresponding,
in order, to:

1 - The tag
2 - The line
3 - The char (point) at which the tag occurs.

You can customize this variable.
