holiday-hebrew-holidays is a variable defined in `holidays.el'.
Its value is 


((holiday-hebrew-passover)
 (holiday-hebrew-rosh-hashanah)
 (holiday-hebrew-hanukkah)
 (if calendar-hebrew-all-holidays-flag
     (append
      (holiday-hebrew-tisha-b-av)
      (holiday-hebrew-misc))))
