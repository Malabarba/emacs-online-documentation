makefile-dependency-regex is a variable defined in `make-mode.el'.

"^\\(\\(?:\\$\\(?:[({]\\(?:\\$\\(?:[({]\\(?:\\$\\(?:[^({]\\|.[^\n$#})]+?[})]\\)\\|[^\n$#)}]\\)+?[})]\\|[^({]\\)\\|[^\n$#)}]\\)+?[})]\\|[^({]\\)\\|[^\n$#:=]\\)+?\\)\\(:\\)\\(?:[ ]*$\\|[^=\n]\\(?:[^#\n]*?;[ ]*\\(.+\\)\\)?\\)"

Regex used to find dependency lines in a makefile.
