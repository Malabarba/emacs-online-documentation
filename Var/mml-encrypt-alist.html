mml-encrypt-alist is a variable defined in `mml-sec.el'.
Its value is (("smime" mml-smime-encrypt-buffer mml-smime-encrypt-query)
("pgp" mml-pgp-encrypt-buffer list)
("pgpauto" mml-pgpauto-sign-buffer list)
("pgpmime" mml-pgpmime-encrypt-buffer list))


Documentation:
