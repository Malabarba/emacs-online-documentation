--- title: "org-agenda-persistent-marks" layout: doc --- org-agenda-persistent-marks is a variable defined in `org-agenda.el'.
Its value is 
nil


Documentation:
Non-nil means marked items will stay marked after a bulk action.
You can toggle this interactively by typing `p' when prompted for a
bulk action.

You can customize this variable.

This variable was introduced, or its default value was changed, in version 24.1 of Emacs.
