--- title: "org-agenda-remove-timeranges-from-blocks" layout: doc --- org-agenda-remove-timeranges-from-blocks is a variable defined in `org-agenda.el'.
Its value is 
nil


Documentation:
Non-nil means remove time ranges specifications in agenda
items that span on several days.

You can customize this variable.

This variable was introduced, or its default value was changed, in version 24.1 of Emacs.
