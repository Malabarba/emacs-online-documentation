org-footnote-re is a variable defined in `org-footnote.el'.
Its value is 


"\\[\\(?:fn:\\([-_[:word:]]+\\)?:\\|\\(?:\\([0-9]+\\)\\]\\)\\|\\(fn:[-_[:word:]]+\\)\\)"

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Regular expression for matching footnotes.
