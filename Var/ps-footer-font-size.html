ps-footer-font-size is a variable defined in `ps-print.el'.
Its value is 
(10 . 12)


Documentation:
Font size, in points, for text in the footer, when generating PostScript.
Either a float or a cons of floats (LANDSCAPE-SIZE . PORTRAIT-SIZE).

You can customize this variable.

This variable was introduced, or its default value was changed, in version 21.1 of Emacs.
