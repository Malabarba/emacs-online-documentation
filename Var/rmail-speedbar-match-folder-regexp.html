--- title: "rmail-speedbar-match-folder-regexp" layout: doc --- rmail-speedbar-match-folder-regexp is a variable defined in `rmail.el'.
Its value is 
"^[A-Z0-9]+\\(\\.[A-Z0-9]+\\)?$"


Documentation:
Regexp matching Rmail folder names to be displayed in Speedbar.
Enabling this permits Speedbar to display your folders for easy
browsing, and moving of messages.

You can customize this variable.
