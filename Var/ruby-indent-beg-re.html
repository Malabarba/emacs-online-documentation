ruby-indent-beg-re is a variable defined in `ruby-mode.el'.
Its value is
"^\\(\\s *\\(?:class\\|def\\|module\\)\\|\\(?:begin\\|case\\|for\\|if\\|un\\(?:less\\|til\\)\\|while\\)\\)\\_>"

This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.
