ses-paramlines-plist is a variable defined in `ses.el'.
Its value is 


(ses--col-widths -5 ses--col-printers -4 ses--default-printer -3 ses--header-row -2 ses--file-format 1 ses--numrows 2 ses--numcols 3 ses--numlocprn 4)

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Offsets from 'Global parameters' line to various parameter lines in the
data area of a spreadsheet.
