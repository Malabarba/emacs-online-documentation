--- title: "texinfo-subsubsection-level-regexp" layout: doc --- texinfo-subsubsection-level-regexp is a variable defined in `texnfo-upd.el'.
Its value is 


"\\(?:appendixsubsubsec\\|subsub\\(?:heading\\|section\\)\\|unnumberedsubsubsec\\)"


Documentation:
Regular expression matching just the Texinfo subsubsection level headings.
