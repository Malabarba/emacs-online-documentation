tty-color-mode-alist is a variable defined in `tty-colors.el'.
Its value is ((never . -1)
(no . -1)
(default . 0)
(auto . 0)
(ansi8 . 8)
(always . 8)
(yes . 8))


This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.
