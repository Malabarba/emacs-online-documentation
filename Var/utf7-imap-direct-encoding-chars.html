--- title: "utf7-imap-direct-encoding-chars" layout: doc --- utf7-imap-direct-encoding-chars is a variable defined in `utf7.el'.
Its value is 
" -%'-*,-[]-}+\\~"

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Character ranges which do not need escaping in the IMAP variant of UTF-7.
