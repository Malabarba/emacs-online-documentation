--- title: "viper-minibuffer-map" layout: doc --- viper-minibuffer-map is a variable defined in `viper-keym.el'.
Its value is 


(keymap
 (10 . viper-exit-minibuffer)
 (13 . viper-exit-minibuffer))

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Keymap used to modify keys when Minibuffer is in Insert state.
