--- title: "xscheme-control-g-disabled-p" layout: doc --- xscheme-control-g-disabled-p is a variable defined in `xscheme.el'.
Its value is 
nil

  • Automatically becomes buffer-local when set.

Documentation:
This variable, if non-nil, indicates that a control-g is being processed
by the scheme process, so additional control-g's are to be ignored.
