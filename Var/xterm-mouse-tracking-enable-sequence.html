--- title: "xterm-mouse-tracking-enable-sequence" layout: doc --- xterm-mouse-tracking-enable-sequence is a variable defined in `xt-mouse.el'.
Its value is 
"[?1000h[?1006h"

  • This variable may be risky if used as a file-local variable.

Documentation:
Control sequence to enable xterm mouse tracking.
Enables basic tracking, then extended tracking on
terminals that support it.
