What is this?

This is not a copy of the emacs manual, or the elisp reference.

This is a list of all functions and variables defined in the latest emacs version, with links to their respective built-in documentations (the documentation you get by calling describe-function or describe-variable).

The information contained here is easily accessible from inside Emacs but is not available online on either of those two sources, that's why I decide to make it available.
